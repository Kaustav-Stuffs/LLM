1.What is the purpose of the SFA (Sales Force Automation) application?
The SFA application helps track client visits, schedule meetings, record expenses, and manage salesforce tasks digitally.
2. How can I log in to the SFA application as a user?
You need your Employee ID, Password, and Company Identifier to log in. Then tap on the "Login" button.
3. Can an Admin/Manager log in with the same credentials format as a user?
Yes, Admins/Managers also log in using their Employee ID, Password, and Company Identifier.
4. How do I start or stop my Outdoor Duty (OD)?
Tap the “Toggle Button” on the Dashboard to start or stop Outdoor Duty.
5. Why is it important to create a meeting for every client visit?
Creating meetings logs your visit and helps calculate travel distance, which is essential for tracking and reporting.
6. How can I create a meeting for an existing client?
Tap on “Meeting”, press (+), select “Existing Client”, choose the client, and enter meeting details after the meeting ends.
7. How can I create a meeting for a new client?
Tap on “Meeting”, press (+), select “New Client”, fill in client details, save, and then submit the meeting details.
8. What does 'Mark Meeting Without Info' mean?
It allows you to mark a meeting at a location without filling client or meeting details. Default info is saved automatically.
9. Can I schedule a follow-up meeting while creating the current meeting?
Yes, follow-ups can be scheduled during the meeting entry process.
10. How can I add meeting-wise expenses?
Go to the Meeting list, select a meeting, tap “Add Expense”, fill in details, and submit.
11. Is it possible to view the route taken during OD meetings?
Yes, each meeting includes a “Route” option that lets you view your OD path using a map.
12. How do I create a new lead or client in the system?
Go to “Leads/Clients”, tap (+), fill in the required fields, and tap “Add Lead/Client”.
13. How can I update an existing lead or client?
Select a lead/client, tap “Edit” on the top right, modify details, and tap “Update”.
14. Can I add branches to an existing client?
Yes, use the “Branch” option while editing a client to add branches.
15. How can I schedule a future meeting?
From the Leads/Clients list, choose the client, tap “Schedule Meeting”, enter details, and submit.
16. Can an Admin/Manager schedule meetings for other users?
Yes, Admins/Managers get a dropdown to assign meetings to users.
17. What is the Follow-Ups section used for?
It allows users to view, reschedule, cancel, or reassign previously scheduled follow-up meetings.
18. Under what conditions can a meeting be rescheduled?
Only if the assigned user hasn't started OD and the meeting time hasn't started.
19. How can I reschedule a meeting?
Go to Follow-Ups, select a meeting, tap “Reschedule”, fill in the details, and submit.
20. How can I cancel a scheduled meeting?
Go to Follow-Ups, select the meeting, tap “Cancel”, provide a cancellation remark, and submit.
21. Can managers reassign meetings to other users?
Yes, from the Follow-Ups section, managers can select a meeting, tap “Re-assign”, choose a user, and submit.
22. How can I add day-wise expenses?
Go to the “Expenses” section, tap (+), enter the expense details, and tap submit.
23. Can I view the status of my submitted expenses?
Yes, users can filter and view approved/rejected expenses in the Expenses section.
24. Who can approve or reject submitted expenses?
Only Managers or Approvers can approve/reject expenses.
25. Can partial approvals be made for expenses?
Yes, but only for individual expenses—not cumulative totals.
26. What information is required to create a new client?
To create a new client, you must fill in details such as Client Name, Contact Number, Email, Address, and optionally GST details.
27. Can I add images or attachments while adding client or meeting details?
Yes, the system supports uploading client images and meeting photos if required by your company.
28. Is GPS tracking mandatory for OD and meetings?
Yes, GPS is used to calculate distance traveled and log actual meeting locations.
29. What should I do if GPS is turned off or unavailable during a meeting?
You should enable GPS manually. If it remains unavailable, the app may not allow you to proceed.
30. What is the role of the Company Identifier during login?
The Company Identifier links the user credentials to a specific organization and is mandatory for login.
31. Can users see a summary of their OD activities?
Yes, a summary or report of OD start and end times, along with meeting count and distance covered, is available.
32. What types of filters are available in the Meetings section?
Users can filter meetings by Date, Client, Status (Pending/Completed), and Meeting Type.
33. What is the use of 'Mark Without Meeting Info'?
It allows field agents to quickly mark their location when client or meeting details aren't available on the spot.
34. Can I edit a meeting after it has been submitted?
No, once a meeting is submitted, it cannot be edited. You may request a manager to review or log a follow-up.
35. How are distance and travel expenses calculated in the SFA App?
The app calculates distance using GPS tracking and links expenses to OD and meetings for accountability.
